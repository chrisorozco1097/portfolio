Facility Services Yearly KPIs

Automated yearly dashboard tracking key performance indicators for Facility Services.

Technologies

Power BI
MySQL
DAX
SharePoint
Power Automate

Objective

This project focused on digitizing and automating the yearly performance reporting process for the Facility Services Department, by integrating operational data from AiM (maintenance management system) and HR-related metrics from Workforce (timekeeping and attendance system).

Historically, this reporting process involved multiple spreadsheets and manual calculations across departments, leading to inconsistencies and delays.
The objective was to centralize all departmental KPIs into a single, interactive Power BI dashboard, providing real-time, filterable insights into both operational efficiency and workforce metrics.

My Role & Techniques

1. Data Integration & ETL Automation

  • Power Automate was used to extract daily CSV files from Workforce (including absenteeism and turnover data) and convert them into JSON format for streamlined ingestion into Power BI.
  • Leveraged MySQL queries to extract live operational data from AiM, including:
    • Work order status
    • Phase completion timelines
    • Maintenance costs
    • Work order classifications (Reactive vs Preventive)

2. Advanced Data Modeling with DAX

  • Designed a robust Power BI data model, joining data from AiM, Workforce, and internal HR sources.
  • Used DAX calculations to:
    • Derive complex KPIs like Absenteeism Rate, Turnover Rate, and Average Days to Complete Work Orders by Priority.
    • Perform dynamic calculations by year, building, shop, and category.
    • Highlight variance from target goals with visual cues (conditional formatting).

3. Interactive Power BI Dashboard

  • Developed an interactive dashboard with:
    • Dynamic filters by Unit, Shop, and Building to drill into specific areas of responsibility.
    • Multiple*KPI cards that highlight key metrics:
      • Average Days to Complete Work Orders (segmented by priority levels: Emergency, High, Routine, Scheduled, Preventive).
      • Absenteeism Rate, comparing Regular Hours vs Leave Hours with industry benchmarks.
      • Reactive vs Preventive Maintenance Ratio visualized through pie charts.
      • Maintenance Cost per Square Foot, allowing cost analysis at both the building and square footage level.
      • Turnover Rate and Exit Reasons Breakdown, including Average Days of Service by separation type.
    • Incorporation of latest refresh date and target indicators for transparency on update frequency.
Interactive KPI dashboard showcasing operational and HR metrics for Facility Services.

Results & Impact

  • Centralized all departmental KPIs into a single automated report, replacing multiple disconnected spreadsheets.
  • Reduced time spent on yearly reporting from several days to zero, with fully automated yearly data refreshes.
  • Enabled leadership to track compliance and performance goals (e.g., response time targets, absenteeism rates) at the end of the year.
  • Increased accountability across shops and units, by providing visibility of individual unit performance.
  • Improved financial oversight with maintenance costs broken down per square foot and by building.
  • Empowered HR and Directors with a turnover and absenteeism dashboard, facilitating early interventions and policy adjustments.

Why It Matters

This project delivers a comprehensive performance tracking tool that bridges the gap between operational maintenance data and workforce management.
By automating data transformation and visualization, it allows Facility Services leadership to:

  • Track performance against goals
  • Monitor absenteeism trends and workforce stability
  • Identify high-cost maintenance areas or inefficiencies
  • Make data-driven decisions on staffing, maintenance planning, and budgeting.

This solution has eliminated the end-of-year reporting bottleneck and introduced a culture of monitoring and accountability, significantly enhancing the department’s ability to improve service delivery while optimizing workforce management.
