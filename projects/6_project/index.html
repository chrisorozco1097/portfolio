Employee Extracts - Payroll Validation

Automated biweekly payroll verification system to ensure accuracy of employee hours and salary rates before payroll processing.

Technologies

Power BI
MySQL
DAX
SharePoint
Power Automate

Objective

The HR Department was responsible for validating payroll extracts received on a biweekly basis, manually reviewing discrepancies in employee rates and hours before final payroll submission.
With over 500 employees and data spread across multiple systems (PeopleSoft, AiM, Workforce), this task was time-consuming, error-prone, and often delayed due to the lack of quick comparison tools.

This project aimed to fully automate the payroll validation process, integrating payroll extracts into a centralized Power BI report, highlighting inconsistencies instantly, and providing actionable insights before errors could affect employee paychecks.

My Role & Techniques

1. Automated Payroll Data Pipeline

  • Developed a Power Automate flow that triggers automatically when the HR Director sends the payroll extract via email.
  • The flow:
    • Extracts the attached payroll files
    • Processes and transforms the data using dynamic expressions
    • Cleans, aligns, and integrates it into an existing Power BI dataset.

2. Biweekly Report Refresh

  • The report dynamically compares PeopleSoft official rates and hours with Workforce clock-ins and AiM records, identifying discrepancies in:
    • Total hours worked
    • Hourly salary rates
    • Supervisor assignments
  • Integrated DAX measures to automatically categorize mismatches and highlight risk areas.

3. Real-Time Feedback Loop

  • After each successful data refresh, the Power Automate flow sends an automated confirmation email back to the HR Director, notifying them that the report is ready for review.
  • Included visual indicators for hours mismatching and rate mismatching, enabling quick decision-making.
Automated Employee Extracts Report comparing PeopleSoft, AiM, and Workforce data.

Results & Impact

  • Reduced manual review time from hours per payroll cycle to under 10 minutes
  • Automated comparison of over 500 employee records every two weeks
  • Proactively identifies payroll issues before payroll submission
  • Automatic notifications ensure the HR team is informed without extra steps
  • Improved payroll accuracy, reduced errors, and faster approval cycles

Why It Matters

Payroll errors directly impact employee satisfaction and organizational trust.
This solution significantly reduced the risk of overpayment, underpayment, and payroll delays by giving HR real-time validation tools and clear visualizations of payroll discrepancies.

The implementation empowered HR to act faster, with more confidence, while eliminating tedious manual tasks. This project represents how automation and reporting can enhance internal controls and contribute to more effective financial management.
