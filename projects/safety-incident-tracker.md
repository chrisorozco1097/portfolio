# 🛡️ Safety Incident Tracker

## 🧰 Technologies  
`Power BI` · `PowerApps` · `MySQL` · `DAX` · `SharePoint` · `Power Automate`

---

## 🎯 Objective  
Previously, the Safety Department used a physical whiteboard to track safety incidents — a process that was manual, error-prone, and lacked historical visibility. This project aimed to fully automate the tracking, submission, and reporting process to ensure data accuracy and real-time access to KPIs.

---

## 🧠 My Role & Techniques

- ### 🔹 Custom Incident Submission App  
  Developed a PowerApps form tailored for the Safety Director to log incidents with key fields (e.g., Shop, Incident Type, and Body Part Affected) to ensure consistent and structured data collection.

- ### 🔹 SharePoint as Data Storage  
  Configured a SharePoint List to store incident submissions, ensuring structured, accessible data across the Microsoft ecosystem.

- ### 🔹 Automated Data Processing with Power Automate  
  Built a scheduled flow that:
  - Extracts incidents daily
  - Categorizes records by month
  - Calculates monthly KPIs (e.g., incident rate, frequency by shop/body part)
  - Prepares data for dashboard consumption

- ### 🔹 Visual Dashboard with Power BI  
  Created an interactive Power BI report featuring:
  - Trend analysis by month and shop
  - KPIs with color-coded indicators
  - Integration with HR system to filter by active employees only
  - Drill-through capabilities for deeper analysis

---

## 📈 Results & Impact

- ✅ Replaced a manual whiteboard with a fully automated digital system  
- ✅ Cut down update and reporting time by **over 90%**  
- ✅ Provided leadership with real-time insights into safety performance  
- ✅ Enabled early detection of recurring incident patterns
